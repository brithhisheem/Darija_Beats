Why Learn Darija? / علاش تتعلم الدارجة؟

Darija is the Moroccan dialect of Arabic 🇲🇦. It’s spoken everywhere in Morocco and mixes Arabic, Amazigh, French, and Spanish influences.

Learning it will help you communicate with locals, understand the culture, and enjoy your travels!
