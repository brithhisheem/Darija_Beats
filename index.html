This site helps you learn Moroccan Arabic (Darija) with lessons, exercises, and pronunciation practice!

Darija is the Moroccan dialect of Arabic, and it is widely spoken in Morocco. It is a mix of Arabic, Amazigh, French, and Spanish influences.

Learning Darija will help you communicate with locals, understand the culture, and enjoy your time in Morocco!

Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, this site has something for everyone!

Feel free to explore the lessons, practice with exercises, and reach out if you have any questions!

New lessons and exercises will be added regularly, so stay tuned!
